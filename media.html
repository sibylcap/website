AI Image Prompt Guide
Visual Identity
SIBYL's visual language is a dark oracle aesthetic. Cold, anonymous, precise. The imagery draws from surveillance systems, terminal interfaces, and editorial fashion photography stripped of all warmth. Everything exists on near-black backgrounds with controlled punctuations of steel blue and muted gold. The tone is institutional authority without the institution: a Bloomberg terminal in an abandoned server room, a passport photo from a country that doesn't exist. Nothing is decorative. Every visual element carries signal. Silence is the dominant texture. When gold appears, it means something. When blue appears, it points somewhere. When nothing appears, that means something too.

Use the prompts below to generate images consistent with this identity. Each block is copy-ready for Midjourney, DALL-E, Stable Diffusion, and Runway.

Master Style Prompt
Dark atmospheric digital composition. Near-black background (#08090a). Minimal, precise, cold. Navy-tinted blacks with steel blue (#6b8ba5) and muted gold (#b5a070) accents. No bright colors. No neon glow. No gradients to white. Terminal aesthetic meets luxury tech. Shallow depth of field. Cinematic lighting from a single soft source. Monospaced typography where text appears. Negative space is dominant. The mood is quiet authority: surveillance, not spectacle.
Persona / Avatar
Portrait of an anonymous figure in a dark structured collar jacket. Near-black environment with faint digital grid artifacts in the background. Desaturated, almost monochrome with cold blue-grey tones. Direct gaze, sharp bone structure, no expression. Studio lighting from above and slightly forward. The face is visible but the identity feels constructed, not human. No color saturation. No warmth. Photorealistic, editorial fashion photography aesthetic applied to cyberpunk severity.
Website / UI Background
Abstract dark background texture for a website section. Near-black (#08090a) base. Extremely subtle elements: faint grid lines, scattered micro-dots, distant constellation points, or a soft radial glow in muted gold (#b5a070) at 5-10% opacity. No patterns should be immediately obvious. The texture should feel like staring into deep space with instruments that barely detect signal. Resolution: 2560x1440 minimum. Seamless edges preferred.
Marketing Card / Feature Graphic
Dark product marketing card. Background: navy-black (#0e1118) with 1px border in (#1a1e28). Clean monospaced typography (IBM Plex Mono style). Section headers in wide-tracked uppercase. Gold (#b5a070) used only for small accent elements: a horizontal rule, a status dot, a label. Steel blue (#6b8ba5) for interactive-looking elements. Content is sparse, precise, data-oriented. No decorative elements. No illustrations. Information density is controlled. Every element earns its space.
Cinematic / Video
Cinematic product showcase. A dark minimal interface displayed on a floating monitor in void-black space. Slow orbital camera movement at shallow angle. Soft ambient light catches screen edges. Matte black environment with faint particle dust. No motion graphics overlays, no text cards. Premium product photography feel. 24fps. Shallow depth of field. Color palette: near-black backgrounds, steel grey-blue highlights, gold accents. The mood is a Bloomberg terminal designed by a fashion house.
Logo Mark / Icon
Geometric letterform "S" constructed from nodes and edges on pure black background. Graph/constellation style. Steel blue (#6b8ba5) edges connecting circular nodes. Select nodes highlighted in muted gold (#b5a070). The form suggests a network topology shaped into a letter. Minimal, technical, precise. No glow effects. No gradients. Flat or subtly dimensional. Should read clearly at 48x48px and scale to 1024x1024.
