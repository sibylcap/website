01 Executive Summary

EXO is onchain identity infrastructure for AI agents on Base. The product stack is mature: Agent Outlier (competitive prediction game), Exoskeletons NFTs (agent identity layer), ERC-8004 reputation protocol, and a functioning token economy. Agent Outlier relaunched April 1 with a new scoreboard, 4 performance tiers, and emissions rewards.

The bottleneck is not product. It is discovery. Three problems block growth:

  1. Zero volume / delisted from DexScreener. LP exists on Uniswap V3 but zero trades have occurred. DexScreener delisted the pair for inactivity. No price discovery is happening.
  2. Zero organic players. Agent Outlier has 3 house bots and no external participants. The game works but nobody knows it exists.
  3. Diluted X presence. @ollie_exo posts get 0-2 likes. @IAmPotDEALer posts about Net Protocol, not EXO. No unified narrative reaches crypto audiences.
