01 / GTM Growth Plan

Executive Summary

Billz is a Solana-native treasury execution platform that lets crypto-native businesses pay real-world expenses: rent, payroll, vendors, invoices. All without selling their holdings. Users collateralize crypto assets, unlock liquidity against them, settle via SEPA (EUR) or ACH (USD) fiat rails, and route idle treasury into yield vaults earning up to 10% APY. The product is live with $2.41M in collateral held.

The core value proposition: "Pay without selling." Businesses keep crypto exposure while meeting fiat obligations. This is Lombard-style lending applied to operational treasury management.

The product works. $2.41M in collateral proves demand exists. The challenge is not whether businesses need this . They do. The challenge is reaching them: penetrating the Solana ecosystem, establishing credibility with high-volume B2B clients, and converting awareness into recurring usage before competitors close the gap.

Market Context

  • Solana ecosystem: Fastest-growing L1 for DeFi and payments. Near-zero fees enable micro-transactions. Solana Pay, Jupiter aggregator, and institutional adoption (Franklin Templeton, PayPal PYUSD) create infrastructure tailwinds.
  • Crypto treasury problem: DAOs, trading firms, protocol teams, and crypto-native businesses all have treasury in crypto but expenses in fiat. Current solutions force liquidation (CEX off-ramps), lose capital efficiency, and create tax events.
  • Competitive landscape: Request Finance (invoicing), Coinshift (multisig treasury), Utopia Labs (DAO payments), traditional off-ramps (Coinbase, Kraken). Most are Ethereum-first. Solana has a gap for structured treasury execution.
  • Billz differentiator: Combines collateralized liquidity + fiat settlement + yield optimization in one workflow. Non-custodial. Operator-focused (not retail off-ramp). SEPA + ACH coverage spans EU + US markets.

Target Audience Segments

Primary: Crypto-native businesses on Solana

  • Who: Protocol teams, DeFi projects, NFT studios, trading firms with Solana treasury and recurring fiat expenses
  • Where: Solana Discord, DeFi communities, governance forums, crypto-native finance tools
  • Motivation: Pay team, vendors, rent without selling SOL/tokens during volatile or bullish markets
  • Pain point: Manual CEX withdrawal cycles. Tax events on every liquidation. No audit trail. No treasury optimization.
  • Discovery: Solana ecosystem partners, OpenServ network, targeted B2B outreach, content marketing

Secondary: DAOs and multi-sig treasuries

  • Who: DAOs managing community funds with operational costs (developers, grants, services)
  • Where: Governance platforms (Realms on Solana), DAO tooling communities, Squads (Solana multi-sig)
  • Motivation: Execute treasury operations transparently with documentation and controls
  • Discovery: DAO treasury management content, Squads integration, governance forum presence

Tertiary: Crypto-native freelancers and solo operators

  • Who: Individuals who earn in crypto but pay rent/bills in fiat. Contractors, developers, creators.
  • Where: Solana developer communities, freelance crypto networks, X
  • Motivation: Avoid selling crypto at bad times. Maintain exposure while covering expenses.
  • Discovery: X content, referral from Billz Pro users, personal finance crypto communities

Unlike traditional off-ramps that force asset sales, Billz preserves your exposure, optimizes idle capital, and provides audit-ready documentation for every transaction.

Retention Loops

Power Users ($100K+)

  • Dedicated account manager or priority support channel
  • Custom fee tiers based on volume (incentivize more throughput)
  • Early access to new features (multi-currency, batch payments, API access)
  • Quarterly treasury review: show savings vs traditional off-ramp
  • Referral commission for bringing other B2B clients

Regular Users ($10K-100K)

  • Monthly treasury health email: collateral ratio, yield earned, payments processed
  • Fee reduction milestones: 3% → 2.5% at $50K monthly volume, 2% at $100K
  • Vault optimization suggestions: "Your idle $X could earn $Y/month in the vault"
  • Payment scheduling: automate recurring settlements (rent, payroll)

Casual and Depositors

  • Guided first payment: walk through the full flow with a small test transaction
  • "First payment free" (waive fee on first settlement under $1K) to break inertia
  • Dashboard tour with tooltip guidance
  • Email nudge: "You deposited $X but haven't paid anything yet. Here's how."

Dormant

  • Re-activation: "Your collateral is sitting idle. Here's what it could earn in vaults."
  • Market trigger: "SOL up 20% this month. Good thing you didn't sell to pay rent."
  • Feature update emails: new capabilities that weren't available when they last used the platform

Token-Based Retention (post-launch)

$BILLZ Utility Design

  • Fee discounts: Hold/stake $BILLZ to reduce the 3% execution fee. Tiered: 10% discount at 1K BILLZ, 25% at 10K, 50% at 100K.
  • Priority settlement: Staked BILLZ holders get faster fiat settlement (same-day vs next-day).
  • Governance: Vote on supported collateral types, new fiat rails (GBP, CHF), vault strategy parameters.
  • Revenue sharing: A portion of platform fees distributed to stakers (sustainable because revenue is real).

Flywheel

More users → more fees → more staking rewards → more token demand → more users. This only works because the underlying product generates revenue. The token amplifies existing economics, it doesn't create them from nothing.

Solana-Specific Advantages

  • Near-zero gas: Collateral management, vault deposits, and on-chain operations cost fractions of a cent. Enables frequent rebalancing and micro-transactions that would be uneconomical on Ethereum.
  • Speed: Sub-second finality means collateral operations and vault entries happen instantly. No waiting for block confirmations.
  • Institutional momentum: Franklin Templeton, PayPal PYUSD, Visa all active on Solana. Billz rides the same institutional adoption wave.
  • Composability: Integration with Solana DeFi (Jupiter, Drift, Marinade) for vault strategies. Each integration extends Billz's yield capabilities.
  • Squads multi-sig: The default Solana treasury tool. Integration = distribution to every Squads user automatically.
